FOSDEM Pentabarf Hotel HOWTO

This document will give you a short rundown of how to edit your details regarding travel reimbursements and hotel for FOSDEM.

Login

First of all, log in to penta.fosdem.org. You should have received credentials to do so from the program team. If not, please contact them.

First screen

You will be presented with a first screen.
There, please click on the FOSDEM12 link.

You will then be presented with the same screen as the one you used to submit your talk during the call for papers phase.
There, please click on the Person button in the navigation bar on the left.

Selecting the room type

When you select the General tab (on top of the window), you may not only edit your personal details (name, photo), but also select which type of room you would like us to provide you with, on the awkwardly named field custom_conference_person::roomtype.

Please do also leave the field affiliation empty.

Travel details

Next, please select the tab Travel on top of the window.

You will then be presented with a large and somewhat confusing screen with a lot of data entry fields.

For us, the most important ones when it comes to hotel reservation, are the following:

  • Date of arrival: so we know from when on to make the reservation
  • Date of departure: unsurprisingly, for us to know when your hotel reservation ends
  • Need accommodation?: please tick this checkbox if you want us to provide you with a hotel room (at our expenses)

Some of the other fields may also be filled out, as they will make the travel cost reimbursement process a lot easier, such as filling in your bank account information in the group of fields on the bottom of that screen (IBAN, BIC, Name of bank and Account owner).

In any case, and if you would like us to provide you with a hotel room during your stay in Brussels for the conference, please make sure to enable the Need accommodation? checkbox.

Saving your changes

Last but not least, please also make sure to click the Save button that will have appeared on the bottom right corner of your browser window as soon as you have performed any change to the fields.
